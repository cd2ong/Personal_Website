Biography

I am a recent graduate from the University of Waterloo from their Systems Design Engineering program. I am very passionate about UX/UI design, graphic design, and human computer interaction. My program focused on the iterative design process and each term had at least one course focused on user experience design. I focused my studies in my last year of university around user experience design, user interface design, and biomedical engineering. There are multiple UX/UI Projects that I worked on in university and for my capstone project I was the lead UX/UI designer for my team (see gallery). For my capstone project I worked with a startup company based out of the Velocity Foundry called Boogaloo Bunks (Boogaloobunks.com) to develop their first prototype for their system. While working with a team of 5 other engineering students, we completed a interdisciplinary project that won 4 major awards (2 design symposium awards, and 2 entrepreneurial awards totaling $11,000 in cash prizes). I also have work experience in full stack development working for various companies and I am a very versatile worker who is proficient in web development, programming, UX/UI design, and graphic design.

Although I am very passionate about UX/UI design and engineering, I also have many other interests. I like to remain active and my preferred sport of choice is volleyball. I like to play competatively and I am currently playing weekly in a casual volleyball night. I also enjoy outdoor activities including hiking, biking, and backpack camping. In my spare time I like to write and perform spoken word poetry and I am also an avid reader. I prefer reading non-fiction but I also enjoy a casual fiction novel ever so often. Some of my favourite authors are Kurt Vonnegut, Joseph Heller, and F. Scott Fitzgerald.
